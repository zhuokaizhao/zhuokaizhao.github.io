Biography
I am currently a last-year PhD candidate in Computer Science at the University of Chicago. During my PhD, I am fortunate to have been working with Dr. Gordon Kindlmann, Dr. Michael Maire, and Dr. William Irvine on various projects related to deep learning, interpretable machine learning, and recommendation systems. Previously, I received my Master's in Robotics from the Johns Hopkins University, under the supervision of Dr. Nassir Navab in 2018, and my B.S in Electrical Engineering from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign in 2016, where I was fortunate to work with Dr. Seth Hutchinson.

I am happy to collaborate on machine learning related projects. Please feel free to send me an email.
